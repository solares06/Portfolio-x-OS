## Overview

Solstice is a sustainable architecture firm that needed a brand identity as considered as their buildings. The brief was to create a system that communicates permanence, environmental sensitivity, and structural elegance.

### The Wordmark

The logotype is set in a custom-modified geometric sans-serif. Subtle optical adjustments — slightly narrowed counters, softened terminals — give it warmth without sacrificing precision.

### Colour System

The palette is derived from natural stone and sky:

- **Sandstone** — Primary warm neutral
- **Slate** — Secondary cool neutral  
- **Lichen** — Accent green for sustainability messaging
- **Dawn** — Warm highlight for premium touchpoints

### Applications

The identity system extends across business cards, architectural proposals, site signage, and digital platforms. Every application follows a strict grid system that mirrors the firm's architectural philosophy.
