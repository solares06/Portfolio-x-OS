## Overview

Aura Meditation is a mobile application designed to bring warmth and tactility to the digital wellness space. In a market saturated with cold, clinical meditation apps, Aura takes a deliberately different approach.

### Design Philosophy

The app's interface draws inspiration from natural materials — warm stone, soft linen, aged paper. Every interaction feels intentional and grounding, from the gentle haptic feedback on button presses to the organic animations that guide breathing exercises.

### Key Features

- **Adaptive colour temperature** — The palette shifts from warm ambers in the morning to cool indigos at night
- **Haptic breathing guide** — Subtle vibrations synchronise with guided breathing patterns
- **Minimal chrome** — No visible navigation bars; the content is the interface
- **Handwritten annotations** — Personal notes appear in a custom hand-lettered typeface

### Impact

- 4.8★ average rating across 12K+ reviews
- 68% daily active user retention at 30 days
