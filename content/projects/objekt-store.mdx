## Overview

Objekt Store is an e-commerce platform for curated premium homewares. The challenge was to create a shopping experience that feels more like browsing a design exhibition than scrolling a product catalogue.

### Design Decisions

- **Grid as architecture** — Products are arranged in an asymmetric masonry grid that creates visual rhythm and discovery
- **Material photography** — Every product is shot against complementary natural backdrops
- **Type-driven hierarchy** — Product names in display serif, descriptions in clean sans-serif
- **Frictionless checkout** — Three-step purchase flow with progressive disclosure

### Technical Details

Built with Next.js and a headless CMS, the platform achieves sub-second page loads through aggressive image optimisation and edge caching. The cart persists across sessions via local storage with server-side sync.

### Results

- 22% improvement in conversion rate vs. previous platform
- 1.4s average page load time
- Average order value increased by 31%
