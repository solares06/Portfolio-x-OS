## Overview

The Gallery Framework is a digital redesign for a contemporary art gallery that needed its online presence to match the reverence of its physical space. The goal was to create an experience where the art — not the interface — commands attention.

### The Challenge

Traditional gallery websites suffer from a common problem: they compete with the art they showcase. Dense navigation, busy layouts, and decorative elements distract from the work that deserves the viewer's full attention.

### The Approach

We stripped the interface to its absolute essentials:

- **Full-bleed imagery** — Artworks occupy the entire viewport, respecting the artist's intended scale
- **Invisible navigation** — Navigation elements appear only on interaction, then dissolve
- **Typographic restraint** — A single typeface at two weights carries all textual information
- **Cinematic transitions** — Slow, deliberate crossfades mirror the contemplative pace of a gallery visit

### Results

- 3.2× increase in average session duration
- 47% reduction in bounce rate
